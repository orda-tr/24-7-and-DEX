Executive Summary

24/7 Traditional Asset Trading — Hedged via DEX Perpetuals

Product Overview
  • Launch 24/7 trading in traditional assets: Oil, Gold, Silver, NASDAQ, S&P 500, major equities
  • Hedge client exposure ~100% using perpetual futures on DEX
  • Hedging logic: Client goes long → we hedge long on DEX (same direction). Net cost ≈ 0
  • Risk materializes when DEX execution diverges (slippage, ADL, oracle failures)
Key Risk Considerations
  • Slippage on Massive Closures: Large simultaneous client exits force hedge closure at unfavorable prices — cost scales non-linearly with NOP size
  • Fast Market / Leverage Mismatch: If hedge leverage exceeds client avg leverage, hedge liquidates first → unhedged window until client SL fires
  • ADL Risk: Insurance fund depletes → hedge forcibly closed — fully unhedged
  • Oracle / Smart Contract Risk: $3.1B lost in DeFi H1 2025 alone
Recommendation
  • Start with conservative exposure caps and phased rollout
  • Keep hedge leverage below client average leverage to ensure client SL fires first
  • Hedge leverage must be carefully balanced: too high → liquidation risk in fast markets; too low → excess capital locked on Hyperliquid exposed to counterparty risk
  • Cap total client NOP per asset based on DEX liquidity profile — illiquid assets (Silver, Gold) require stricter NOP limits to contain slippage cost on hedge closure
  • Real-time monitoring of insurance fund health, oracle integrity, slippage thresholds
  • Pre-defined circuit breakers and manual intervention protocols
